In 1954, against a backdrop of McCarthyism, six strangers are invited to a party in a secluded New England mansion.
They are met by the house butler, Wadsworth, who reminds them each that they have been given pseudonyms to protect their true identity.
During dinner the seventh attendee, Mr. Boddy (Lee Ving), arrives.
