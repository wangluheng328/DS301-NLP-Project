Holden and Banky are best friends and authors of a popular comic book called "Bluntman and Chronic".
Holden falls in love with Alyssa, also a comic book artist, who happens to be a lesbian.
Holden's new relationship with Alyssa strains his life-long friendship with Banky, and soon the relationship between Holden and Alyssa hits a wall due to Holden not being able to deal with her past.
