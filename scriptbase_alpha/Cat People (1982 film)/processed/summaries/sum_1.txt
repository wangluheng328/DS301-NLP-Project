Irene Gallier, a beautiful young woman is on the bridge of sexuality; she discovers love for the first time only to find that the explosive experience brings with it tragic consequences.
However, the tremendous passion of this girl's first romantic love is so strong, it bypasses the terror around her - including her brother's extraordinary demands - as it pushes her on to her own bizarre destiny.
Desire... passion... blood, her lust transforms her into one of the Cat People.
