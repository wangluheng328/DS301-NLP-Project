In a coastal North Carolinian small town in the mid 1990's, a boy from the popular but troubled undirected group of students gets busted, and for punishment, you guessed it, has to do community service activities which include the high school's spring play.
This throws him in with the minister's daughter, you guessed it, the mousy seemingly awkward yet beautiful girl with an angelic heart, and she sings too.
They grow hesitantly closer than their previous adversarial relationship as old bonds are tested and new awarenesses are inspired.
A couple twists occur as the story concludes.
