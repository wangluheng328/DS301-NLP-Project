Darth Vader and the Empire are building a new, indestructible Death Star.
Meanwhile, Han Solo has been imprisoned, and Luke Skywalker has sent R2-D2 and C-3PO to try and free him.
Princess Leia - disguised as a bounty hunter - and Chewbacca go along as well.
The final battle takes place on the moon of Endor, with its natural inhabitants, the Ewoks, lending a hand to the Rebels.
Will Darth Vader and the Dark Side overcome the Rebels and take over the universe?
