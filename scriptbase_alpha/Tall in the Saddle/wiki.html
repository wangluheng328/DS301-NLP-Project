Tall in the Saddle is a 1944 Western film starring John Wayne as a misogynist cowboy and Ella Raines as his feisty lady-rancher love interest. It is the only movie pairing of Wayne and Raines. The screenplay was co-written by actor Paul Fix and the film was directed by Edwin L. Marin.

The film was Wayne's second of a six-picture contract with RKO and the first with the producer Robert Fellows, with whom Wayne later formed a production company.
