Aaron's father unexpectedly passes away, and he is called upon to inform his entire extended family to attend the funeral.
After the funeral home delivers the wrong body, things appear to calm down.
Relatives and friends pour in - including a grouchy wheel-chair bound uncle; Aaron's New York-based selfish brother - an unsuccessful writer; drug-impaired cousins; and a petite stranger who no one seems to know - who has an agenda of his own.
