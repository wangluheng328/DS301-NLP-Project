Legend has it that a tortured soul called Davy Jones lives beneath the sea with a terrible sea beast at his command.
Unfortunately for the notorious pirate, Captain Jack Sparrow, this particular legend is very real.
Years ago, Jack made a deal with Davy Jones, who is now expecting him to make good on his promise by handing himself over to Jones's servitude.
But Jack will not give in without a fight, which is especially bad news for Will Turner and Elizabeth Swann, who always seem to get caught up in Jack's mishaps.
If Jack can't find a way to rid himself of Davy Jones once and for all, it may just spell the end for the three adventurers
