Major Deakins is a tough U.S. Air Force pilot who is assigned to a flight mission with 'nice-guy' Captain Hale.
They are to fly the B3 Stealth Bomber on wargame maneuvers over Utah loaded with two live nuclear weapons.
Deakins, having been continually passed over for promotion, sells out his country and arranges to use the thermonuclear warheads under his command to blackmail the U.S. government.
The only thing standing in his way is his old buddy, Captain Hale....
