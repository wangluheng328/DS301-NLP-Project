Zion is falling under siege from the machine army.
In a matter of hours 250,000 Sentinels, programmed to destroy mankind, will attack and yet the citizens of Zion, encouraged by Morpheus, are placing their faith in The Oracle's prophecy that The One will end the machine war and restore peace.
But can Neo live up to their expectations?
He may have new powers, including the ability to fly, but his recurring visions of Trinity's death are jeopardising his chances whenever he's in The Matrix...
