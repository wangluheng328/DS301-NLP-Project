After an anonymous killer guts two high school lovers, a mysterious rise in fear and rumors rise in a peaceful Californian town.
A killer starts killing high school teenagers, with a young teenage girl, an eccentric and dramatic cop, and a selfish and ambitious reporter trying to investigate the murders.
A strange number of events starts to happen around Woodsboro, including an accused killer, a suspicious boyfriend, discovered love and attraction, and, of course, the mysterious killer with a ghost face mask.
Suspense, cleverness, and gore.
