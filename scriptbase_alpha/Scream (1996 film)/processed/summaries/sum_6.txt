A quiet, peaceful town in California becomes dangerous after the raping and slaying of Mrs. Prescott, mother of teenage daughter Sidney Prescott, occurs.
Almost a year later, 2 of Sidney's classmates are found dead and gutted where everybody in the town is a suspect.
After Sidney is attacked and manages to escape, Sideny's boyfriend Billy seems to have the red paint on his hands.
But when he's proven innocent, a confused and upset Sidney tries to find who really is causing all the past murders ... and soon to come murders.
