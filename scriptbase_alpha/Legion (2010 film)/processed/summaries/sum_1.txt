God loses faith in mankind and orders Angel Michael in addition to a legion of other angels to bring the Apocalypse to Earth and exterminate the human race.
However, Michael rebels against the order and decides to help the humans.
Meanwhile in a diner in the middle of nowhere, Bob Hanson, runs the place with his son Jeep, a pregnant waitress, Charlie, and his friend Percy Walker.
Their customers, the Anderson family, are stranded until Jeep can fix their BMW and lonely Kyle Williams who is lost.
