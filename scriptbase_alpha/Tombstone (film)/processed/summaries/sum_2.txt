A hollywood historical account of Tombstone, Arizona's famed "Shootout at the O.K.
Corral" and the events that led up to it.
Tension between "the Law" and "the Cowboys" stirs as the Cowboys, led by "Curly Bill" Broscius, accuse the Earps (Wyatt, Virgil, and Morgan) and their outlaw/gambler associate "Doc" Holliday of interefering with their operations inside and outside of town.
