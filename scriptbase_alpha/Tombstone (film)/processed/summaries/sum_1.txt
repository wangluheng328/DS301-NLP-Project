Former lawman Wyatt Earp, his two brothers and friend Doc Holliday settle in the town of Tombstone in order to lead a profitable and peaceful life but soon come into conflict with the Cowboys, a band of outlaws led by the Clanton brothers.
This retelling of the Earp/Clanton feud also follows up the gunfight at the O.K.
Corral with a look at the bloody consequences of the showdown.
