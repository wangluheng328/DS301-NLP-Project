White Angel (1994 film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

White Angel is a 1994 British thriller film directed by Chris Jones and starring Harriet Robinson, Peter Firth and Don Henderson.[1] A crime writer is approached by a serial killer who wishes her to tell his story to the world.
