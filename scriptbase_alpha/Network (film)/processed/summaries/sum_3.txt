A fourth network is struggling for ratings and turns its news division over to the entertainment division.
As one of the ramifications of this move the news Anchor is fired.
He goes on the air with a wonderfully daffy rant and rave session culminating in his insisting that people go to the windows and yell, "I'm as mad as hell, and I'm not going to take it anymore."
His ravings make him an Icon as the need to sell begins to overwhelm everyone touched by the network.
