Andy Dufresne, is sent to Shawshank Prison for the murder of his wife and secret lover.
He is very isolated and lonely at first, but realizes there is something deep inside your body, that people can't touch and get to....'HOPE'.
Andy becomes friends with prison 'fixer' Red, and Andy epitomizes why it is crucial to have dreams.
His spirit and determination, leads us into a world full of imagination, filled with courage and desire.
Will Andy ever realize his dreams?
