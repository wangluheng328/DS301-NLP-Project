The Fugutive is on the run!
Dr. Richard Kimble and Sam Gerard race through this manhunt movie.
Prison escapee Dr. Richard Kimble is a Chicago surgeon falsely convicted of killing his wife and determined to prove his innocence by leading his pursuers to the one-armed man who committed the crime.
Sam Gerard is an unrelenting bloodhound of a U.S. marshal.
They are hunted and hunter.
Their nonstop chase has only one exhilarating speed: all-out.
