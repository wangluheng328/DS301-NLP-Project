The dazzling, refurbished USS Enterprise soars proudly once again in this ultimate space adventure.
When an unidentified alien destroys three powerful Klingon cruisers, Captain James T. Kirk (William Shatner) returns to the newly-transformed USS Enterprise to take command.
Shatner is joined on this heroic mission by Leonard Nimoy, DeForest Kelley, and the cast from the acclaimed "Star Trek" television series.
The entire crew mobilizes at warp speed to stop the alien intruder from its relentless flight toward Earth.
