In Colorado, the lonely Milton hunts down a gang of worshipers of a satanic cult trying to find where their leader Jonah King is.
King has killed Milton's daughter and abducted her baby to sacrifice him at midnight of a full moon in Stillwater and Milton wants to rescue his granddaughter.
Milton reaches Oklahoma and finds that Stillwater is a deactivated prison in Louisiana.
Then he helps the waitress Piper first with her damaged car and then he protects her against her brutal boyfriend.
In return, she gives a ride to him to Louisianna.
Soon they discover that a mysterious stranger called The Accountant is chasing them.
What is the secret of Milton and The Accountant?
