British Ministry secret agent John Steed, teams up with Emma Peel to stop Sir August De Wynter from destroying the world with a weather changing machine.
They are called in to investigate Prospero, a plot to blackmail Britain by controlling the weather.
Emma Peel is suspected of being involved but it is discovered that she has a double who works for the real baddie, Sir August de Wynter.
He threatens to freeze Britain, at one point unleashing a blizzard on Trafalgar Sqaure.
