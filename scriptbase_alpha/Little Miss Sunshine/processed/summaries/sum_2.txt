The Hoover family is the dictionary definition for the word "dysfunctional".
The dad Richard is a man who gives lectures on winners and losers, the wife is Sheryl, a chain-smoking, frazzled wife and working mother whose idea of a home cooked meal frequently consists of a bucket of chicken.
Her gay brother Frank recently attempted suicide.
The grandpa is Edwin, a drug addict.
The son is Dwayne a rebel who has vowed not to talk until he gets into the Air Force.
And then there is Olive, a seven-year old girl who dreams of going to the Little Miss Sunshine pageant.
So what happens when they do?
