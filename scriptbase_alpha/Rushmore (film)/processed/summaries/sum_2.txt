Max Fischer is a more than determined student at his prep school, Rushmore Academy where he oversees anything with the word "extra-curricular".
He befriends a preschool teacher named Ms. Cross, whom he ends up falling in love with.
Only to find things take a turn for the worst when his mentor, Herman Blume, an unhappy millionaire falls for her as well.
As a result, their personal and professional lives spiral out of control.
