Sixty-three year old Harry has a reputation for dating girls a third his age.
Taking Marin, his latest belle, back to her beach house for the weekend he is taken aback when her mother Erica unexpectedly turns up.
Suffering a heart attack while romancing Marin, Harry finds himself housebound with Erica his nurse and only companion.
As they grudgingly get to know each other something starts to stir, though as the hospital doctor who treated him has taken a shine to Erica, Harry could have serious competition.
