I Still Know takes place one year after the original movie.
Julie's room mate - Karla - wins a trip for four to the Bahama's.
Karla, her boyfriend and Julie with her new boyfriend - Will - go on vacation.
While singing on stage, Julie follows the words for the song on a screen, but she then sees the words 'I STILL KNOW' flash across the screen.
Julie is now trapped on the island with the hook-wielding killer who knows what she did last summer...
