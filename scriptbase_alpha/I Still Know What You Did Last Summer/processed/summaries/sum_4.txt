Two years after the horrible incident, Julie is back in college and is worse than ever having dreams about the killer.
She gets comfort from a new friend named Will.
Julie's other friend Karla tries to hook the two of them up and when she wins a trip to the Bahamas, she knows that it is perfect.
But Ray is still in the picture and Julie only goes as friends.
Karla, her boyfriend Tyrell, Julie, and Will take flight right before Ray encounters the fisherman again.
He tries to tell Julie not to leave but it is too late.
Now in the Bahamas Julie is starting to lighten up and have fun.
Then suddenly things start going terribly wrong when a storm hits the Island and people start to disappear.
Ray takes a boat to the island to warn Julie but will he make it on time to save Julie again from the fisherman?
