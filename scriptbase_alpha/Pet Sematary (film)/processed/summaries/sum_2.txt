Pet Sematary (PS) is a 1989 horror flick based on Stephen King's novel of the same name.
After moving into their new home the Creed family's cat is killed after wondering onto the highway.
Jud an elderly neighbor shows Louis, the father, to an isolated hill behind the local Pet Cemetery and instructs him to bury the deceased feline there.
Not long after the cat reappears at the Creed home, only he is not the same.
The docile cat is now vicious and destructive.
When their youngest son meets with a fatal accident, the distraught Louis buries him in the same location hoping to revive him.
Unfortunately he unleashes far more than he had bargained for.
