Anakin's wife Padme is pregnant, but he is having visions of her dying in childbirth.
Meanwhile, a Sith Lord is manipulating the Council, Obi-Wan is chasing the robotic separatist leader General Grievous, and Yoda helps the Wookies defend themselves against the droid armies on Kaschyyyk.
Will Obi-Wan succeed, will Padme survive, and most importantly: how does Anakin become Darth Vader?
