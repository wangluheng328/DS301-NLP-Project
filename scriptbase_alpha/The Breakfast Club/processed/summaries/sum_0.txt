They were five students with nothing in common, faced with spending a Saturday detention together in their high school library.
At 7 a.m., they had nothing to say, but by 4 p.m., they had bared their souls to each other and become good friends.
To the outside world they were simply a Brain, an Athlete, a Basket Case, a Princess, and a Criminal, but to each other, they would always be the Breakfast Club.
