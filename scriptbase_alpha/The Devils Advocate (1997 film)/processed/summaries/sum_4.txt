Defence attorney Kevin Lomax has never lost one single case.
He is invited to New York to work for a big law firm ruled by senior board member John Milton, who showers him with wealth and feeds his vanity.
Kevin's wife just wants to have a baby and is distressed by Kevin always being on a case and never at home.
She starts to lose her grip on reality - or so it seems.
Kevin Lomax is about to find out about his true heritage and the grand scheme John Milton is running from his penthouse apartment.
