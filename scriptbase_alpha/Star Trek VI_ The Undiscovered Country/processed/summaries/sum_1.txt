The Enterprise leads a battle for peace and a lunar cataclysm bring the Klingons to their knees.
After seventy years of war, the Federation and the Klingon Empire prepare for a peace summit.
Ironically, it is Captain James T. Kirk who is the first emissary to broker that peace.
But the prospect of intergalactic glasnost with sworn enemies is an alarming one.
When a Klingon ship is attacked and the Enterprise is held accountable, the dogs are war are unleashed again, as both worlds brace for what may be their final, deadly encounter.
