Sean Archer has been chasing Castor Troy to avenge his son's death by the hands of the psychotic terrorist for six years.
Now that he killed Castor, Sean must find a biological bomb by assuming the identity of Castor Troy by taking his face and being brought in prison.
But Troy, who awakes from his coma, takes Archer's face in order to take revenge on him.
Now, Troy turns Archer's life upside down by assuming his identity.
Will Archer break free from his prison and get his face back, or will Troy have the last laugh ?
