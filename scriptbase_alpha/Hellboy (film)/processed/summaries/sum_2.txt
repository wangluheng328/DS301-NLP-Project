When the Nazis attempt to use black magic during the last days of World War II, a monkey like demon is thrown into the real world.
Trevor Bruttenholm discovers the creature and rescues it from any danger.
The creature grows up and goes by the name of Hellboy.
Working in secrecy, Hellboy works to rid any evil.
When some familiar faces from the past appear, it's going to take more than Hellboy to stop them.
