In 2055, earth is no longer viable enough for food production.
A promising algae experiment on Mars runs into trouble, so a small Air Force-civilian scientific-technician team travels there.
The emergency landing is a nearly-fatal disaster: provisions losses and equipment threaten both survival and return capacity.
Even AMEE, a navigation drone, is accidentally switched into its dangerous military mode.
As if that weren't bad enough, errors, suspicions and emotions like guilt make the men on mars an woman on watch in the ship behave erratically.
Yet some startling discoveries and Robby Gallagher's inventiveness rekindle hope, at least for earth.
