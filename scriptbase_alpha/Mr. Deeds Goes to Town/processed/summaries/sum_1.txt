The heir of wealthy, deceased Martin Semple proves surprisingly to be one Longfellow Deeds of Mandrake Falls, Vermont.
The bemused lawyers in the land of taciturn Yankees find Deeds a simple-hearted greeting card poet, his favorite pastime playing the tuba.
Will he be any match for city slickers, embezzlers, moochers, fundraisers, and phonies?
Enter brash reporter Babe Bennett, who (for a story) gains Deeds' friendship and seems to be on his side.
With friends like these...
