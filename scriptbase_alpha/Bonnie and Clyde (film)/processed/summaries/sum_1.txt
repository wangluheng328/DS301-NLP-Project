Bonnie Parker is bored with life and wants a change.
She gets her chance when she meets a charming young drifter by the name of Clyde Barrow.
Clyde has dreams of a life of crime that will free him from the hardships of the Depression.
The two fall in love and begin a crime spree that extends from Oklahoma to Texas.
They rob small banks with skill and panache, soon becoming minor celebrities known across the country.
People are proud to have been held up by Bonnie and Clyde; to their victims, the duo is doing what nobody else has the guts to do.
To the law, the two are evil bank robbers who deserve to be gunned down where they stand.
