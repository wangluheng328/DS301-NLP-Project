Frank and Jack Baker are two piano players and brothers.
Moreover, they play together in small pubs and clubs all over the country for 15 years.
But when their business is not doing so well, they decide to join a woman to their shows.
After the auditions, the best candidate they could find was Susie Diamond, and from now on their shows become very successful.
But when Jack and Susie are having an affair, the fabulous Baker brothers are not getting along anymore, and the little band is about to break down.
