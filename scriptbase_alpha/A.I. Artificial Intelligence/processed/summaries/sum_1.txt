In the futuristic tale, a highly advanced robotic boy named David is 11 years old.
He weighs 60 pounds.
He is 4 feet, 6 inches tall.
He has brown hair.
His love is real.
But he is not.
He is a marvel of cybernetic progress who really only wants to be a real boy, loved by his mother and his father in that happy place called home.
But David journeys out into the forests to find a way to become a real boy.
