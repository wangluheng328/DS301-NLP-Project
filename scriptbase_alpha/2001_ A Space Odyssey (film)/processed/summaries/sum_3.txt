The monoliths have been watching us.
They gave humankind the evolutionary kick in the pants it needed to survive at the Dawn of Time.
In 1999, humankind discovered a second monolith on the moon.
Now, in the year 2001, the S.S.
Discovery and its crew, Captains Dave Bowman and Frank Poole, and their onboard computer, HAL 9000, must discover what alien force is watching Earth....
