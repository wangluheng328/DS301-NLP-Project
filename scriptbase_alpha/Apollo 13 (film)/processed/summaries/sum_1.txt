A movie based on what was to be the third lunar-landing mission.
This film shows the trials and tribulations of the Apollo 13 crew, mission control, and families after a near-fatal accident cripples the space vehicle.
A mission that couldn't get TV airtime because space flights had become routine to the American public suddenly grabbed the national spotlight.
This is a tale of averted tragedy, heroism and shows a testament to the creativity of the scientists who ran the early space missions.
