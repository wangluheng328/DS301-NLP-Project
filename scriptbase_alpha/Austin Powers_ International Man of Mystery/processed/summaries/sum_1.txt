A 1960's British hipster secret agent Austin Powers is brought out of cryofreeze to oppose his greatest arch-enemy Dr.
Evil into the 1990s.
30 years later, Austin was then released and is now thawed back into action to once again battle Dr.
Evil where he wakes up somewhere in Nevada and steals a nuclear weapon and holds the world hostage.
After Austin is woken up to stop Dr.
Evil he gets a new sexy sidekick Miss Vanessa Kensington, his ex-partner's daughter and they go to Vegas where they must stop Dr.
Evil's outrageous plot to control the world for 100 billion dollars!
