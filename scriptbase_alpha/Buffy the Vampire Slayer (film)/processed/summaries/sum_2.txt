Buffy is an air-head cheerleader who is chosen by a guy named Merrick to fend the world of Vampires.
When Merrick first informs Buffy that she is the "chosen one," she thinks he's crazy, but then strange things begin to happen.
First, Buffy's friend Cassandra and many other kids form her school turn up missing and are later found "dead" with bite marks on their necks.
After many other strange ocurrences, Buffy then realizes that those bite marks were made by vampires, and that they really are out there.
She comes to to horrifying truth that she is the "chosen one."
