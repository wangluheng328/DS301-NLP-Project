Alex and his senior french class are going on a trip to France in the spring.
Alex is very nervous, and has a deadly premonition that the plane he is on will explode after take off.
Alex and six others, including his teacher, get off the plane.
Soon after, the plane explodes.
Now his teacher and friends are starting to avoid him.
Shortly after school ends, Alex and his friends are being hunted down by Death, angry that they cheated the "design".
It doesn't take long for Alex to realize that you can't cheat death!
