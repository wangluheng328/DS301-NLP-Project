A TV weather man reluctantly goes to cover the story of Punxsutawney Phil for the third year in a row.
Making no effort to hide his frustration about this, he covers the story and moves on, expecting his job to be finished.
However, he awakes the 'following' day and discovers that it's Groundhog Day again, and the fun happens again, again, and again.
First he finds this be burdensome and unavoidable no matter what, then he soon realizes he must take advantage of it in order to secure the love of a coworker.
