The Witching Hour
Directed by Henry Hathaway
Starring Guy Standing
Release dates April 26, 1934
Running time 69 min.
Country United States
Language English

The Witching Hour is a 1934 film directed by Henry Hathaway. It stars Guy Standing and John Halliday. [1]

Cast[edit]

  • Guy Standing as Judge Martin Prentice
  • John Halliday as Jack Brookfield
  • William Frawley as Jury Foreman
  • Judith Allen as Nancy Brookfield
  • Tom Brown as Clay Thorne
