Reed Richards is a very talented scientist seeking the funding for a space experiment.
Along with his friend and partner Ben Grimm, he goes to businessman Victor Von Doom and manages to get his support.
So, Reed and Ben, along with Doom, his partner Sue Storm and her brother Johnny, find themselves aboard Doom's private space station.
However an accident will flood the station with cosmic rays and Reed, Ben, Sue and Johnny will gain amazing powers thus becoming the Fantastic Four.
But, the four of them aren't the only ones affected by the cosmic rays...
