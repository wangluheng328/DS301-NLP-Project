After the confrontation with Chris Higgins, Jason Voorhees presumed dead, is taken to the Wessex County Morgue.
There he escapes killing an attendant and a nurse, making his way back to Camp Crystal Lake.
Six teenagers rent a cabin next to the Jarvis' cabin in Camp Crystal Lake.
Soon Jason finds his way to eliminate all who trespasses his way, but not for long.
There are two survivors left, a girl and her little brother, Tommy Jarvis.
The final battle will define their destiny, be prepared for the Final Chapter.
